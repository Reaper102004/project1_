Welcome to Valorant

Valorant, developed by Riot Games, is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) that has captivated gamers worldwide since its release in 2020. The game blends precise gunplay with unique agent abilities, creating an exciting and strategic experience. Whether you're an experienced FPS player or new to the genre, Valorant provides a challenging and rewarding environment for all.

Valorant is a team-based game where players take on the role of "agents," each with unique abilities. The primary objective in the game is to either plant or defuse a bomb (called the "spike") or eliminate the opposing team.
